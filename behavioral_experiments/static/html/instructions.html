Building Game!

In this experiment you will play a fun communication game with another player on MTurk! Each game consists of 12 trials. On each trial you will work together to build structures like the one below:

One of you will act as the "Architect" and the other will be the "Builder". Every trial will include the following steps:

STEP ONE: The Architect will see the "target structures, the Builder will only see a blank grid.

STEP TWO: The goal of the Architect will be to help the Builder construct the target towers. The Architect will use the chat window to instruct the Builder how to buld. The Architect does not need to give all the instructions at once. Also note that the sketcher and viewer will not be shown the exact same images. For instance, a group of five owls might appear to each player in a different configuration, such as:

The Sketcher will have 140 characters to create their message. Once they are done, they will click “send message”.

STEP THREE: The message will be shown to the Builder, who will then place as many bloacks as they want onto the grid. As each block is placed, the Architect will see it appear on their screen. Once a block is placed it cannot be moved.
When the Builder has finished, they can click the "End Turn" button, and the Architect will be able to send another message if they wish, and repeat the process until 8 blocks have been placed.

STEP FOUR: When all 8 blocks have been placed, the Architect can end the round by pressing the "End Round" button. The Builder will then be able to see the target outline, and a BONUS will be allocated. Partial credit will be given even if a block is not perfect, so keep trying even if there is a mistake! Then the next trial will begin.

For each correct block Builder places, both of you will receive an accuracy bonus of $0.02. In addition, you will both receive a bonus of $0.01 for partially correct blocks..

The bonus meter below displays the total Bonus accumulated.

A few final notes: First, since you are playing with another player, you may see this screen before the game begins: Just hold tight. Another player should join the game within the next few minutes. If you're in the waiting room for more than 5 minutes without another player joining, please return the HIT and try again later! Otherwise, once a Viewer joins, you will be notified and need to click a button to start the game.

Second, to make sure your work is properly recognized, we ask that you be respectful of your partner and the rules of the game. If you are the Architect, try to be clear. If you are the Builder, please do your best to be be as accurate as possible so you can both earn the highest possible bonus.

Third, please DO NOT refresh the page at any point once you start the game (including the waiting page). If you refresh, you will not be able to rejoin the game you were in and you may not be able to submit the HIT.
Thank you!

In sum:

As an Architect, your priority is to send useful information via the message bar that will help the Builder place blocks correctly. After each message, the Builder must place at least one block, so please provide useful messages.

As a Builder, your job is to place at least one block each round
